About Us

Hi. My name is Zuber Shaikh. I am a Mumbai based photographer. I am into photography since 1995 when photography was new and smartphones weren’t there.

I knew I wanted to become a photographer when I first held camera in my hand and have seen my own clicked photographs. The feeling of capturing moments was just beautiful. And I haven’t stopped since then.
Here at GOODLUCK DIGITAL PHOTO STUDIO we just not take pictures but we create memories. In the last 25(many) years we’ve been a part of hundreds of weddings, events, photoshoots and more, and we are continuously growing and evolving each day.

We will be more than happy to be a part of you special day and make it more memorable for you. Contact Us
